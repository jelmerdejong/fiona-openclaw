## Learnings

1. **Haiku will fake success.** Always verify execution. Silent failures in unattended cron are common.
2. **Exec has three layers:** `tools.exec.security` (master), `ask` (prompts), `approvals` (grants). Security is the gatekeeper.
3. **Isolated sessions are sandboxed.** `sessions_list` from isolation can only see itself. Use `exec ls` and `read` instead.
4. **Telegram group long-polling issue.** May be IPv6 timeout or OpenClaw forum/topic group limitation. Needs investigation.
5. **Obsidian vault enrichment works.** Wiki-links and structured metadata create navigable knowledge bases at scale (451 books → 402 pages).
